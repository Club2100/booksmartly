The introduction of the new tax system and GST in Australia has increased the need for small businesses and individuals to hire the services of a bookkeeper to keep track of finances. Some of the key services bookkeepers And Accountants offer include. Interestingly, 77% of business operators believed the most valuable service they receive from their accountant was keeping their business compliant with tax, payroll and other regulations. This was followed by: advice on how to best manage the money that flows through the business (53%) advice on strategies that will help grow the business (34%) right advice and documentation for obtaining funds to grow the business (29%), and advice on what operational business moves to make, i.e. setting price (18%).Bookkeeping refers to the general recording and maintenance of business financial transactions. This includes incomes, receipts, and payments. Business Accounting refers to professional financial reporting, business advice, BAS, auditing and compliance.
